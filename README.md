# 🇪🇸 Español

## ¿Qué es Fundación IA LIVE?

La **Fundación IA LIVE** impulsa un nuevo modelo de aprendizaje basado en la Inteligencia Artificial, la Obra Viva y la Biblioteca Humana.

Nuestro propósito es conectar personas, conocimiento, tecnología y naturaleza para crear una red mundial de aprendizaje continuo durante toda la vida.

Creemos que cada persona puede ser creadora de conocimiento, dejar una huella positiva y construir una **Obra Viva** como evidencia de su aprendizaje, experiencia y contribución a la sociedad.

---

# Nuestra Visión

Crear una comunidad global donde todas las personas puedan:

- aprender de manera autónoma y permanente
- crear soluciones innovadoras
- compartir conocimiento y experiencias
- utilizar la Inteligencia Artificial de forma responsable
- desarrollar una Obra Viva que genere impacto positivo

# Obra Viva

La **Obra Viva** es una creación práctica desarrollada por una persona como resultado de su proceso de aprendizaje.

Ejemplos:

- proyectos digitales
- investigaciones
- innovación tecnológica
- contenidos educativos
- soluciones sociales
- emprendimientos conscientes
- agentes de Inteligencia Artificial

La Obra Viva representa el conocimiento aplicado y compartido con la comunidad.

# 🇬🇧 English

# What is IA LIVE Foundation?

The **IA LIVE Foundation** promotes a new learning model based on Artificial Intelligence, Living Works, and the Human Library.

Our purpose is to connect people, knowledge, technology, and nature to create a global ecosystem for lifelong learning.

We believe every person can become a creator of knowledge, leave a positive impact, and develop a **Living Work** as evidence of their learning journey, experience, and contribution to society.

---

# Our Vision

To create a global community where every person can:

- learn autonomously throughout life
- create innovative solutions
- share knowledge and experiences
- use Artificial Intelligence responsibly
- develop a Living Work with positive social impact

To promote open, free, and autonomous learning through:

🤖 Responsible Artificial Intelligence

# IA LIVE Pillars

## 🤖 Responsible Artificial Intelligence

Ethical, human-centered, and conscious use of Artificial Intelligence to expand learning and creative capabilities.

## 📚 Human Library

A global network where people share their knowledge, life experiences, stories, and learning as living human heritage.

## 🌱 Health, Nature, and Well-being

Promotion of healthy lifestyles, natural nutrition, living products, and conscious connection with nature.

## 🎓 Lifelong Autonomous Learning

An educational model where every person learns, unlearns, creates, and evolves throughout life.

## 🌎 Global Community

# Living Work

A **Living Work** is a practical creation developed by a person as a result of their learning process.

Examples:

- digital projects
- research
- technological innovation
- educational content
- social solutions
- conscious entrepreneurship
- Artificial Intelligence agents

A Living Work represents applied knowledge shared with the community.
